WELCOME To GUESS GAME

In this game I i.e. Mr. Computer will select a number between 0-100 and your task is to guess that number but there is a catch, you will get only 7 chances to do so. Don't worry I will help you out by guiding you i.e. I will tell you if your guessed number is smaller or bigger than my selected number. Press 'Start Game' when ever you are ready.
